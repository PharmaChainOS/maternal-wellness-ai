How It Works

Four gentle steps
to daily clarity.

No long forms. No confusing dashboards. Just a calm two-minute ritual that grows more insightful the longer you use it.

01

Log your daily state

A two-minute morning check-in captures how you slept, how you feel emotionally, and any physical changes you noticed. Honest and simple. No clinical language anywhere.

02

Receive your insight

Bloom reads your inputs alongside your trimester data and recent patterns, then surfaces a clear and personal summary of what your body is doing right now and why.

03

Follow your guidance

Receive one or two micro-recommendations shaped to your exact state today. A stretch. A breathing prompt. A reminder to rest. Always matched to your body, never to a textbook average.
